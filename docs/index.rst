How to Access Your Account
==========================

Follow these general steps to sign in:

#. Open your preferred web browser.
#. Go to the official PC Matic account login page.
#. Enter your registered email address.
#. Type your account password.
#. Select **Sign In**.
#. Wait for your account dashboard to open.

After logging in, you can access all available account management features.

Password Recovery
=================

If you forget your password, you can usually recover access by following these steps:

#. Select **Forgot Password**.
#. Enter your registered email address.
#. Check your inbox for password reset instructions.
#. Create a new secure password.
#. Return to the login page and sign in.

Choose a strong password that includes uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special characters.

Common Login Issues
===================

Incorrect Login Details
-----------------------

Verify that:

* Your email address is entered correctly.
* Your password is accurate.
* Caps Lock is turned off.

Browser Problems
----------------

If the login page does not work properly:

* Clear your browser cache.
* Delete browser cookies.
* Try another browser.
* Update your browser to the latest version.

Internet Connection
-------------------

A stable internet connection is necessary for successful login.

Temporary Account Lock
----------------------

Multiple unsuccessful login attempts may temporarily prevent access. Wait a short period before trying again.

Keeping Your Account Secure
===========================

Protect your account by following these recommendations:

* Use a unique password.
* Change your password regularly.
* Never share your login credentials.
* Log out after using a shared computer.
* Keep your recovery email address updated.
